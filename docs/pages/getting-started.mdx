# Getting Started

Get started with Noreva in just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

1. **A Solana Wallet** - We recommend [Phantom](https://phantom.app/) or [Solflare](https://solflare.com/)
2. **SOL for gas fees** - A small amount of SOL (0.01-0.1 SOL) for transaction fees
3. **USDC or SOL** - To swap for tokenized stocks

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [noreva.markets](https://noreva.markets)
2. Click the **"Connect"** button in the top right
3. Select your wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
4. Approve the connection in your wallet

## Step 2: Browse Tokenized Stocks

Once connected, you'll see a list of available tokenized stocks:

- **Token Price** - Current price on Solana DEXs
- **Stock Price** - Real-time price of the underlying stock
- **Spread** - Premium (+) or discount (-) vs the stock
- **Volume** - 24h trading volume

Click on any stock to see detailed charts and trading options.

## Step 3: Start Trading

1. Click on a stock to open the trading modal
2. Choose **Buy** or **Sell**
3. Select payment token (USDC or SOL)
4. Enter the amount
5. Review the quote and click **Swap**
6. Confirm in your wallet

That's it! Your tokens will appear in your portfolio.
