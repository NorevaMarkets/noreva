# Welcome to Noreva

**Trade tokenized stocks on Solana. 24/7.**

> **Ready to trade?** Go to [noreva.markets](https://noreva.markets) to start trading now!

Noreva is a web application for tracking and trading tokenized stocks on the Solana blockchain. Compare real-time prices between tokenized stocks and their underlying securities, analyze spreads, and trade when traditional markets are closed.

## What is Noreva?

Noreva bridges traditional finance with decentralized finance by providing:

- **Real-time Price Tracking** - Monitor tokenized stocks like AAPLx, TSLAx, NVDAx and compare them to their underlying stock prices
- **Spread Analysis** - See the premium or discount between token and stock prices
- **24/7 Trading** - Trade via Jupiter DEX anytime, even when traditional markets are closed
- **Stock Favorites** - Mark your favorite stocks with a ‚≠ê for quick access
- **Portfolio Management** - Track your holdings, trade history, and P&L with Phantom or Solflare wallet
- **Trading Dashboard** - Comprehensive analytics with PnL tracking, performance charts, and trade calendar
- **Market Data** - Fundamentals, news, analyst ratings, earnings calendar, and insider trading data
- **User Accounts** - Secure wallet-based authentication with profile settings
- **Mobile Optimized** - Responsive design with mobile-friendly TradingView charts
- **Smooth Animations** - Polished UI with page transitions and stagger effects
