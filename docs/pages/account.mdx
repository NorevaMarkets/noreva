# Account

Manage your profile and account settings.

## Authentication

Noreva uses **wallet signature authentication** - no passwords required!

### How It Works

1. Connect your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
2. **Automatic authentication** - You'll be prompted to sign immediately after connecting
3. Your wallet signs a message (this is NOT a transaction - no fees!)
4. Noreva verifies the signature to prove you own the wallet
5. You're authenticated for 24 hours

### What Authentication Enables

- **Trade History** - Your trades are saved and linked to your wallet
- **Account Settings** - Save your profile information
- **Favorites** - Your favorite stocks are saved (Note: favorites work even without authentication!)

## Account Settings

Access your account settings by:

1. Connect your wallet
2. Click your wallet address in the header
3. Select **Account Settings**

### Profile Information

You can save the following information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **First Name** | Your first name |
| **Last Name** | Your last name |
| **Email** | Contact email (optional) |
| **X (Twitter)** | Your X handle |
| **Website** | Your personal website |
| **Bio** | A short description about yourself |

Your profile is linked to your wallet address and stored securely in our database.

## Trade History

All trades you make through Noreva are automatically saved to your account.

### Viewing Trade History

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Scroll down to **Trade History**
3. See all your past trades

### Trade Information

Each trade record includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Type** | Buy or Sell |
| **Symbol** | Token symbol (e.g., TSLAx) |
| **Amount** | Number of tokens traded |
| **Price** | Price per token at time of trade |
| **Total** | Total USD value |
| **Status** | Pending, Confirmed, or Failed |
| **Date** | When the trade was made |
| **TX Signature** | Solana transaction signature |

## Data Privacy

- Your profile data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database
- Only you can access your own data (enforced via Row Level Security)
- Your wallet address is your unique identifier
- We never store your private keys
- Trade history is linked to your wallet for your convenience

## Signing Out

To sign out:

1. Click your wallet address in the header
2. Click **Disconnect**

This will clear your authentication token and disconnect your wallet.
