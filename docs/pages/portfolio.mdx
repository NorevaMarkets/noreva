# Portfolio

Track and manage your tokenized stock holdings.

## Viewing Your Portfolio

1. Connect your wallet
2. Click **Portfolio** in the navigation
3. See all your tokenized stock holdings

Your portfolio shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Stock** | Token symbol and name |
| **Balance** | Number of tokens you hold |
| **Token Price** | Current market price |
| **Value** | Total USD value of holding |
| **P&L** | Profit/Loss (coming soon) |

## Portfolio Metrics

### Total Value

The sum of all your tokenized stock holdings in USD.

### Holdings Count

Number of different tokenized stocks you own.

## Supported Tokens

Your portfolio automatically detects any tokenized stocks in your wallet from supported providers:

- **Backed Finance** (bAAPL, bMSFT, bNVDA, bTSLA, etc.)
- **Swarm Markets** (SPYx, QQQx, etc.)

> ℹ️ **Note:** Tokens must have liquidity on Jupiter to display accurate prices.

## Managing Your Holdings

### Adding to a Position

1. Go to the stock detail page
2. Click **Buy**
3. Enter the amount to add
4. Confirm the swap

### Reducing a Position

1. Go to your Portfolio
2. Click on the holding
3. Click **Sell**
4. Enter the amount to sell
5. Confirm the swap

### Closing a Position

To fully close a position, sell your entire balance of that token.

## Wallet Integration

Noreva works with popular Solana wallets:

- **Phantom** - Most popular Solana wallet
- **Solflare** - Feature-rich alternative

Your portfolio data is fetched directly from the blockchain - we never store your private keys or have access to your funds.
