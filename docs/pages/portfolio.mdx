# Portfolio

Track and manage your tokenized stock holdings with comprehensive analytics.

## Viewing Your Portfolio

1. Connect your wallet
2. Click **Portfolio** in the navigation
3. See holdings, trading dashboard, and recent trades

Your portfolio shows:

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Wallet Info** | Connected wallet address and balances |
| **SOL/USDC Balance** | Your SOL and USDC balances |
| **xStocks Holdings** | All your tokenized stock holdings |
| **Trading Dashboard** | PnL, charts, calendar, and stock breakdown |
| **Recent Trades** | Latest 5 trades with quick overview |

## Trading Dashboard (NEW!)

The Trading Dashboard provides comprehensive analytics for your trades with three tabs:

### Overview Tab

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Realized P&L** | Total profit/loss from closed positions |
| **Total Volume** | All-time trading volume |
| **Buy/Sell Volume** | Separate buy and sell totals |
| **Total Trades** | Number of executed trades |
| **Avg Trade Size** | Average USD value per trade |
| **Success Rate** | Percentage of confirmed trades |
| **Most Traded** | Your most frequently traded stock |

The **Performance Chart** shows:
- **P&L Tab**: Cumulative profit/loss over the last 30 days
- **Volume Tab**: Monthly buy/sell volume bars

### Stocks Tab

Detailed breakdown per stock you've traded:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Avg Buy Price** | Average price you paid |
| **Avg Sell Price** | Average price you sold at |
| **Total Bought** | Tokens purchased and USD value |
| **Total Sold** | Tokens sold and USD value |
| **Realized P&L** | Profit/loss for this stock |
| **Current Holding** | Tokens still in wallet |

Click on any stock to go to its detail page.

### Calendar Tab

Visual calendar showing your trading activity:

- **Green dots** - Days with buy trades
- **Red dots** - Days with sell trades
- **Click a day** - View trades from that date
- **Monthly stats** - Buy/sell counts and volume for the month
- **Navigation** - Browse previous/next months

## Holdings Display

Each holding shows:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Logo** | Stock logo (locally cached) |
| **Symbol** | Token symbol (e.g., TSLAx) |
| **Balance** | Number of tokens you hold |
| **Token Price** | Current market price |
| **Value** | Total USD value of holding |
| **Spread** | Current premium/discount vs stock |

Click on any holding to go to the stock detail page.

## Recent Trades

The latest 5 trades are shown below the dashboard:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Type** | Buy (green) or Sell (red) |
| **Symbol** | Token traded |
| **Amount** | Tokens and price |
| **Total** | USD value |
| **Status** | Confirmed, Pending, or Failed |
| **Date** | When the trade occurred |

> **Tip:** For full trade history and analytics, use the Trading Dashboard tabs above!

## Managing Your Holdings

### Adding to a Position

1. Go to the stock detail page
2. Click **Buy**
3. Enter the amount to add
4. Confirm the swap

### Reducing a Position

1. Go to your Portfolio
2. Click on the holding
3. Click **Sell**
4. Enter the amount to sell
5. Confirm the swap

### Closing a Position

To fully close a position, sell your entire balance of that token.

## Wallet Integration

Noreva works with popular Solana wallets:

- **Phantom** - Most popular Solana wallet
- **Solflare** - Feature-rich alternative

Your portfolio data is fetched directly from the blockchain - we never store your private keys or have access to your funds.

## Data Storage

- **Holdings** - Read directly from blockchain (real-time)
- **Trade History** - Stored in Noreva database (linked to your wallet)
- **No custody** - Your tokens remain in your wallet at all times
